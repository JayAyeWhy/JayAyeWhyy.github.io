Out Loud Slang

  1. Chill: To relax or calm down.
  2. Lit: Something exciting or excellent
  3. Dope: Really cool or impressive.
  4. Sick: Awesome or amazing.
  5. Chillin': Relaxing or taking it easy.
  6. Ship: To support a romantic relationship between two people.
  7. Snack: A good-looking person.
  8. Woke: Socially aware or conscious.
  9. On point: Something well done or perfectly executed.
  10. Fleek: On point, perfectly groomed or styled.
  11. Wildin': Acting crazy or out of control.
  12. Slay: To do something exceptionally well.
  13. Throw shade: To criticize or express contempt subtly.
  14. High-key/Low-key: High-key means something done openly or with emphasis, while low-key is the opposite, done subtly.
  15. Vibes: Atmosphere or feelings given off by a person, place, or thing.
